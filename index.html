Greek Autumn

Top 5 Autumn Destinations

Greece is not only a place for summer and winter. Our country's beauty expands, also, in autumn, with its beautiful colors, its amazing environment and its hospitality (as always).

Malvasia

Our beautiful Malvasia in Peloponnese,a place that looks like paradise. A walk to the house of the important Greek poet with over a hundred poetry collections and compositions will make you feel even more the atmosphere of the area. If you don't want to move away from the castle town and prefer to bathe in a place with history, Portelo makes it easy for you. After the last renovation of the area, you too can swim in the historical waters, staying close to the enchanting atmosphere of the castle town.

Rhodes

Historically, Rhodes was famous for the Colossus of Rhodes, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, but, unfortunately for our culture, it got destroyed by an earthquake. The Medieval Old Town of the City of Rhodes has been declared a World Heritage Site. Moreover, in the center of the island of Rhodes there is a small paradise with centuries-old plane trees and abundant running water that create a refuge of coolness and rest for the island's tourists, the 7 springs of Rhodes.

Kerkini Lake

If you are an active person looking for fun activities to do in autumn, it is worth visiting Kerkini lake. The best you can do there are bird watching, riding your bike, sailing on the lake by canoe, boat tour and not only. If you love animals and nature, you will definitely love this lake.

Arcadia

One of the most renowned areas in the Peloponnese, Arcadia, an area of authentic beauty, offers a trail linking historic local settlements that is truly worth exploring. The Menalon Trail, stretching 75km and formed back in antiquity, connects the villages of Stemnitsa, Dimitsana, Zygovisti, Elati, Vytina, Nymfasia, Magouliana, Valtetsiniko and Langadia.

Ioannina

There you can visit the Cave of Perama. It is located in the village of Perama and is only four kilometers from the center of the city of Ioannina.
